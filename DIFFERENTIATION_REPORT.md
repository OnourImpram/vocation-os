## Positioning

Most career tools optimize matching, document generation, tracking, or submission volume.

VocationOS asks a stricter sequence of questions:

1. Is the claim supported and permitted for this output?
2. Is the evidence current enough for this decision?
3. Which route preserves the best portfolio of future options?
4. Is the action reversible and within the approved scope?
5. Did a trusted source confirm that the action completed?
6. What outcome was actually observed under which model and policy version?

## Defensible Advantage

The intended advantage is a Career Digital Twin linked to evidence, opportunities, decisions, documents, approvals, actions, proofs, and outcomes.

This release establishes those contracts and safety boundaries. Market leadership remains a benchmark target until reproducible baseline runs satisfy the published thresholds.
