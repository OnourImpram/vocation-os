## Positioning

Most job tools optimize for speed, matching, or volume. VocationOS optimizes for defensible action. The central question is not “can this be automated?” but “what evidence, reversibility, stakes, and authorization make this action acceptable?”

## Safety Claim

The system blocks unsafe automation when evidence is weak, reversibility is low, stakes are high, ToS status is false, the kill switch is engaged, a packet claim is private, or human authorization is absent.
