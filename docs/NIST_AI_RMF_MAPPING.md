# NIST AI RMF Mapping

Source: https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework

This mapping targets NIST AI RMF 1.0. Because NIST states that AI RMF 1.0 is being revised, this document should be reviewed before every major release.

## Govern

Policies define public scope, private state boundaries, contribution rules, and non negotiable safety gates.

## Map

Modes identify reversibility, evidence needs, high stakes flags, and user authorization requirements.

## Measure

Tests, schema validation, red team cases, privacy scans, brand scans, evaluator checks, and golden outputs measure expected behavior.

## Manage

Kill switch, packet validation, action ledger, blocked attempt records, and release gates manage residual risk.
